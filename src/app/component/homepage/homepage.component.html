Abstract

Living in highly populated cities like Bengaluru, traveling and exploring the city becomes a passion but finding a good and reliable parking place is challenging. Additionaly, Bengaluruâ€™s traffic is always high compared to other metro cities and sometimes, the parking slots are filled in such a way that it is difficult to find a new parking slot. Therefore people are trying to avoid using their own vehicles. Hence a good parking system becomes essential in all areas. Our project aims to develop a smart parking System that can keep track of the parked vehicles and provide hassle free parking for the public using cloud storage. Using this system, the user will be able to find an available slot easily using a mobile application. Updates for parked vehicles are also provided by email and SMS services. It also aims to promote the use of this smart parking system in all public places to give a hassle free completely automated experience for the users. People can have a good trip around the city without worrying about parking slots or long waits in search of one. As it is completely Automated and Organized, It also offers safe and reliable parking for Users.
